Richard T. Wang

About

Hi, I'm Richard! I am currently a 2L at Cornell Law School. I graduated from Washington University in St. Louis with a B.A. in political science and a minor in computer science. I am originally from Sammamish, Washington, and am deeply interested in the intersection of politics, law, and technology. In my spare time, I like to hike, swim, fund the horror cinematic genre, and discover new music. This is a landing page for my work, where I also push the bounds of minimalism.
